FoodWiz

AI-powered Recipes Custom Meal Generation Real-time Ingredient Analysis

FoodWiz is an AI-driven platform that generates recipes based on the ingredients you have. Simply input your ingredients, and FoodWiz will suggest a recipe you can prepare.

It's a quick and easy way to make the most of what you have in your kitchen, helping you cook meals without needing extra ingredients.

QuizWiz

AI-powered Quiz API Integration Real-time Analysis

QuizWiz is an intelligent quiz generation platform that harnesses the power of AI to generate questions, offer real-time feedback, and analyze user performance.

The platform enables users to generate a variety of questions, including multiple choice, true/false, and open-ended questions, with instant validation of answers.

Angell Bike Ecommerce Store

Full-Stack Development Product Management System Responsive Design

Angell Bike is an online platform I developed from scratch, designed to provide a smooth and intuitive shopping experience for electric bikes. I implemented key ecommerce features such as a dynamic product listing page, detailed product descriptions, and a seamless checkout process.

The store is built using modern web development technologies, with a focus on responsive design, ensuring a smooth user experience across devices.
